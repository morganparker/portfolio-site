BLOCBOX
Information collecting and displaying
in an organized platform

THE PROBLEM

People want to connect. They want to share, create and store. Often times information collecting sites do not easily allow for collaboration. We need an easy to use interface for collecting information, storing it and collaborating with others. This should appeal to leisure browsers, professionals, and collective groups.

THE SOLUTION

Blocbox is a SaaS web application which allows people to gather information and store it in one, organized place. Users can create documents, store images, link to various websites, and invite and collaborate with others.

THE USER

Blocbox can be used by anyone. It is versatile and practical. Although Blocbox is super user friendly, our primary user will be the working professional, between the ages of 25 and 34. Blocbox has a modern feel to the outdated bookmarking ways of the past. Because it allows for collaboration, this application can be extremely useful for those working with others on projects, working remotely, and sharing information quickly in one place.

SURVEYING THE USER

We surveyed our typical user to find out how people store and collect information. We found that most people are using their laptops to browse for content, with smart phones being a close second. Social media sites were the main platform for storing and sharing information. Most users are merely bookmarking the content in their browser, with a portion using applications such as Pinterest. Users wanted auto-save features, better functionality, more creativity, and cross-off features. They also liked the visibility of their current application and the editable nature of it.
