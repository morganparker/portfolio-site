ABOUT

HI, THIS IS ME.

My name is Morgan Parker and I love good design. I started my design journey in 2014, after graduating with a Bachelor's Degree in Human Service Studies and spending a year with the Americorps VISTA program. After working some different social service jobs, I decided to go back to school and pursue something in the creative sector. That is where I found graphic and web design.

I obtained an Associates from the Art Institutes in graphic/web design and also completed a 72 week long bootcamp in UX Design and Frontend Development. I have done some freelancing on the side and try to keep up to date with my skills through various online bootcamps and trainings (Skillcrush, Bloc, Lynda, Make It In Design, etc.).

My goal is to help clients bring their creative visions to life. Whether that is through logo development, product photography, print design, web design, or some other need, I love seeing people happy and hopeful for their brand. Besides design, my other greatest loves are my two children and wonderful husband. They are the reason I began this wonderful road toward creative fulfillment, and I enjoy every second I have with them.
